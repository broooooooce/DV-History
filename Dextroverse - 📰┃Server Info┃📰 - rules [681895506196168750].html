1. Be respectful. No racism, homophobia, bigotry, hate, or being a coconut. We want everyone to feel welcome in this community. We understand that some people are okay with certain words, but this is not true for everyone. 2. We are a harm reduction server. Always and forever. We do not want you or anyone else on this server to be harmed by drug use. Please don’t give bad advice. Please do not offer information unless you are 100% sure of its authenticity. 3. Do not source drugs whatsoever. Linking to sites that sell drugs, attempting to sell drugs, or describing how to obtain drugs is not permitted. The only exception is if you are linking to a country’s government run website that sells substances that are legal in the country of question as these websites will only ship to residents of said country. 4. Discussion of illegal activity is subject to penalty. Discussion of illegal activity involving harm of other people (theft, robbery, scamming, terrorism, violent crime) past, present, or planned that is deemed serious and/or unremorseful will elicit ONE strict warning before a ban; depending on the crime, a ban may be dealt outright. 5. Limitation on hard drug discussion. Do not discuss hard drugs in the #lounge. The meaning of "hard drug" varies depending on the person, but for our purposes, "hard drugs" refer to opiates/opioids of the strongest variety, amphetamines (especially methamphetamine), cocaine, and benzos (when used beyond medicinal doses). Those with severe dependence are significantly and holistically disabled and widely stigmatized for good reason. Use of DPH should also never be encouraged. When confronted with people discussing these substances, please react with sensible harm-reducing information. As a DXM-themed server, we want discussion to be mostly DXM (or dissociative/psychedelic) focused: that's our expertise. Please take discussion of hard drugs to #drugs. Those with expertise in these substances should browse #drugs to assist people there who may have questions. Discussion of the pharmacology and science behind hard drugs is permitted, but Officers and Archons reserve the right to ask you to stop discussion at their discretion. (edited)
6. We are an 18+ community. We are a community that discusses adult themes and as such, we ask that you are 18 years of age or older. Individuals who are under 18 are NOT allowed on this server. Period. If you’re found to be under 18, you will be removed. No exceptions. The reason for this is because we do not want our members to have to worry about modifying conversation they wish to have to suit a younger audience. Furthermore, supporting drug use in minors probably isn't the best idea. 7. Seek professional help in emergencies! We are not professionals in the medical field. We cannot save you from an overdose, we cannot talk you through an emotional crisis, and we cannot save your life in an imminent threat. The Suicide Crisis Hotline in the US is: 1.800.273.8255. (edited)

You may not show grenades, ammunition, or firearms. Please ensure these are not visible in the background while you are on video. You may not take screenshots or audio recordings of people without their permission. Rules in Voice Chat are more relaxed than the rules in text chat. 9. All rule enforcement is at the discretion of the Officer and Archon core. We make exceptions and interpret things differently based on context and circumstance. If you are asked to moderate your behavior, you are expected to do so the first time you are asked. It is up to the officers and archons to decide if your actions are acceptable.
