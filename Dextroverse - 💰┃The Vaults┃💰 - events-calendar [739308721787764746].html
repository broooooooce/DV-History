Welcome to the Events Calendar! Here you will find important information about upcoming events that will be taking place in the next 7 days on our server. The events scheduled consist of (but are not limited to) group trips, movies, game nights, server meetings, and eventually special events and contests! You may be wondering... How can I schedule an event? Easy! In this channel there will be a schedule for the 7 days of the upcoming week. You may look at the schedule and if you see an open time for an event you would like to host, send a mod a message and we can book the time off for you! What if I want to plan something a month or so in advance? For big plans like this, we will have a google calendar to keep up with planned events outside of a week. To reserve a large event that takes a lot of planning, you should contact a mod and give them the details. Be ready to answer any questions we have for you! What if something happens and I cannot host an event I have scheduled? If you book a time off to show a movie in cinema, please do your best to be there to host it. If an emergency pops up or something happens and you know you won’t be able to host anymore, let me know and I can do my best to either host it or find someone else who is willing to. Is there a limit to how many events I can schedule? All things in moderation! Don’t bite off more than you can chew, but if you are regularly hosting events and are doing a good job with it, we will not have any objections to you hosting multiple events in a day. Some things to remember -When hosting an event, be cooperative with the mods. There are many things that go into place to make things like this happen. -When attending or taking part in an event, be respectful of the host and all other members. For movies, don’t spoil it! For games, do your best to keep it fun for everyone, and for group trips, don’t be a coconut!

Information Needed To Schedule An Event When scheduling an event, please include the following: Cinema Event- Timeslots are blocked off into 3 hour intervals to allow members adequate time to set up, start movies, and allow people to join. There is a 30 minute break in between each time slot in the case of someone wanting to play a movie right after yours. Please send the name of your movie or show, the time of the movie, and a brief description. This will be used for people who are subscribed to the Down for Cinema role. Game Event- Send the game and the time you would like to host it. This could be an online game, mobile game like Evil Apples, or a pc or console game. If the game has a limit as to how many can play, it is a first come, first serve basis. For contests, group trips, ands special VC events, please message me or @Pasta so we can discuss it with the mod team and correlate with you. Also, please try to schedule more than a day in advance. It is first come, first serve, so waiting until the last minute could result in your spot being taken! Thank you!
