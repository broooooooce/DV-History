DejaBruce 26-Oct-20 11:23 AM
NEW SIMPLIFIED ROLE SYSTEM TO BE IMPLEMENTED IN COMING DAYS As many of you are already aware, we've been working on a way to make the roles simple and intuitive. We've been listening and based on feedback and our own needs as staff, and after a few weeks of discussion on specifics, we've ironed out what we think will be a welcome change to most: Summary: Plateau ranks will be eliminated entirely. Archon will no longer be an xp-based rank. Instead, it will be all of the current 3rd and 4th plats; Archons will retain the ability to mute (or send to the timeout table we're trialing atm), but not promote. The DXM nerds will be Dexperts again, and dextromancer will be repurposed as the top tier xp-based role. The new hierarchy will simply be this (and this is how they will appear on the sidebar): Dexecutive (may be renamed) > Archon (awarded) > Dextromancer (xp) > Self Aware (xp) > Autonomous (xp) > Robot (xp) The MEE6 level requirements for each of the xp based roles will be: Robot: Level 5 Autonomous: Level 25 Self-Aware: Level 40 Dextromancer: Level 50 This means that picture permissions for the #lounge will remain the same. We will not be altering how custom-roles function right now. The color scheme of the xp roles will change. When they do, we'd appreciate your feedback, because nothing is set in stone. In that same breath, we may add another awarded role in the near future, and will be making note of our current 2nd and 1st plat users, to that end. However, we wanted first to move away from a system that has been biased toward favortism over participation, and second, to just get it done. Also, we've heard the feedback about returning the xp based roles to the sidebar, that's going to happen. We hope this makes everyone's experience more intuitive and enjoyable! (edited)

DejaBruce 20-Dec-20 02:12 PM
We've made the preferred pronoun roles self-assignable using the old-school text-based .roles command. (Type .roles in #bot-spam to see the full list of self-assignable roles; .role [rolename] assigns, .derole [rolename] removes.) We have fixed UBER-BOT to not send duplicate left-the-server messages and disabled its usage in #lounge to reduce spam. We've also (re)enabled the following commands that may be used in the other channels: .8ball (virtual magic 8 ball), .beer (facts about specific brands), .choose (decides between two things), .cocktail (provides recipes for mixed drinks), .compliment ("compliments" you), .define (defines a word), .g (googles a thing), .imdb (gives imdb link and summary for movies), .img (post a random image of whatever thing), .meme (use .meme list, then .meme [memename] "top text" "bottom text"), .seen (shows when a user last sent a post), .shorten (shortens a url), .stock (provides stock price, works with crypto), .thesaurus (provides synonyms for a word), .translate (translates TO a language from another; example: ".translate en c'est la vie" returns "That's life"), .twitter (retrieves last tweet from a user), .weather (gives weather based on city or zip code), .wiki (links wikipedia article), and .wolfram (solves math problems, even complex ones; handy for quick dose calculations). You may type .help [commandname] (without the brackets) to get the proper syntax for any of these. Existing commands such as .gif, .insult, .poll, .quickpoll, .quote, .serverinfo, & .userinfo still work as before. You may also now set reminders (outside of #lounge) by typing sentences such as: remind me in 60 minutes that I should be feeling it now. Hope you all find these useful! <3 @Server Update Subscriptor
